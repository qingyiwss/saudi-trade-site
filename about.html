About Hebei Steel & Manufacturing

Rooted in Hebei's 333 industrial clusters, dedicated to serving Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030

Your Direct Bridge to Hebei Manufacturing

Hebei Steel & Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is headquartered in Hengshui, Hebei Province — the industrial heartland of northern China. We are not a trading company or intermediary. We are a direct manufacturer and authorized export partner for factories across Hebei's 333 specialized industrial clusters.

Our mission is simple: eliminate the layers of middlemen that traditionally separate Saudi buyers from Chinese factories. When you work with us, you communicate directly with production teams, negotiate factory-direct pricing, and receive products that are fully certified for Saudi import under the SABER and SASO regulatory frameworks.

Since our founding, we have focused exclusively on the Saudi Arabian market. This singular focus means we understand the Kingdom's business culture, regulatory requirements, logistics preferences, and — most importantly — the ambitious scale of Vision 2030 projects that demand reliable supply chains.

The Power of 333 Industrial Clusters

Hebei's industrial ecosystem is unmatched in scope and specialization. Each cluster represents decades of accumulated expertise in a specific product category.

🏗️

Anping — Wire Mesh Capital

Anping County alone hosts over 600 wire mesh factories, producing 80% of China's wire mesh exports. From architectural mesh for NEOM to industrial filtration for petrochemical plants, this cluster has it covered.

🔩

Yongnian — Fastener Hub

Yongnian District is one of the world's largest fastener production bases, with an annual output exceeding 4 million tons. Every bolt, nut, and coupler that leaves our facility benefits from generations of metallurgical expertise.

🧵

Shijiazhuang — Textile Center

Hebei's capital region has been a textile manufacturing center for over a century. Modern mills now produce technical fabrics including our thobe-grade cotton-polyester blends using computerized looms for consistency.

💄

Cangzhou — Cosmetic Tools

The Cangzhou-Qingxian corridor produces over 70% of the world's makeup brushes. This cluster's mastery of synthetic bristle technology and precision assembly is unmatched globally.

Tianjin Port: Your Gateway to Saudi Arabia

Tianjin Port is northern China's largest port and one of the world's top 10 container ports by throughput. Our factories in Hebei are all within 1,000km of Tianjin, giving us a significant logistics advantage over manufacturers in southern China.

We run consolidated container loads on a weekly schedule. From Tianjin to Dammam's King Abdulaziz Port, typical transit time is 22 to 28 days. We handle all export documentation including the SABER Certificate of Conformity, commercial invoice, packing list, and bill of lading — so your cargo clears Saudi customs without delay.

For urgent orders, we offer LCL (less than container load) options and can coordinate with your preferred freight forwarder in Saudi Arabia. Every shipment is trackable from departure to arrival.
