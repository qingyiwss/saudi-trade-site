Q: What handle materials are available for makeup brushes?

Handle lengths typically range from 140mm to 190mm. We can customize handle shape (round, hexagonal, flat, tapered), thickness, color gradient blending, and surface finish according to your brand specifications.

Q: What ferrule types do you use for makeup brushes?

We use high-quality aluminum ferrules, available in multiple finishes: nickel-plated (classic silver), gold-plated, rose gold, matte black, copper, gunmetal, and champagne gold. Our standard ferrule uses a double-crimp construction — the bristles are first compressed and aligned into the ferrule, then a secondary mechanical crimp secures them with industrial-grade epoxy resin adhesive. This double-crimp + epoxy method virtually eliminates bristle shedding and ensures the ferrule remains firmly attached to the handle. Ferrules are available in round, flat, and oval cross-sections. Custom embossing or laser engraving of your brand name/logo on the ferrule is available with MOQ 1,000 units.

Q: How do you prevent makeup brush bristle shedding?

We use a proprietary 5-step anti-shedding process: (1) Bristles are bundled and precisely trimmed to uniform length using laser-guided cutting equipment; (2) The bundle is inserted into the ferrule and secured with an initial mechanical crimp; (3) High-temperature epoxy resin is injected into the ferrule base and cured for a full 48 hours in a temperature-controlled environment; (4) Every brush undergoes a 24-hour soak test in a solvent solution to identify any loose bristles; (5) A final pull-test applies 2.5 kg of tension — any brush losing more than 3 bristles is rejected. This rigorous process achieves a shedding rate of less than 0.1% in real-world use, meeting the quality standards expected by professional makeup artists.

Q: How do you test makeup brushes for quality?

Our quality control process includes multiple rigorous checkpoints throughout production. Incoming material inspection: bristle fiber diameter measurement (micrometer), length uniformity check, color consistency evaluation, handle material hardness test. In-process inspection: ferrule crimping integrity verification, epoxy fill-level measurement, handle-to-ferrule alignment check (laser-guided). Finished product sampling: AQL 2.5 standard inspection for visual defects — scratches, misalignment, finish inconsistencies, loose components. Performance testing: shedding pull-test (2.5 kg tension, pass/fail at ≤3 bristles lost), 24-hour solvent immersion test, 500-cycle ferrule bend fatigue test, bristle flexibility and shape retention after washing. Packaging inspection: barcode scan verification, print quality assessment, box crush test. We provide a detailed QC inspection report with every shipment.

Thobe Fabric

Q: What fabric composition do you offer for thobe material?

Our thobe fabric is available in multiple compositions optimized for the Saudi climate and wear preferences: (1) 65% Polyester / 35% Cotton — our most popular blend, offering excellent wrinkle resistance, durability, breathability, and a crisp white appearance; (2) 80% Polyester / 20% Cotton — maximum wrinkle resistance with a consistently sharp white look, ideal for daily business and professional wear; (3) 100% Cotton — premium natural fiber feel with maximum breathability for hot Saudi summer days, though requiring more ironing care; (4) Cotton-Linen blend — lightweight summer-weight fabric with a subtle natural texture favored for casual and weekend thobes. All fabric compositions are 100% pure white with excellent colorfastness ratings (Grade 4–5 on the ISO 105 wash fastness scale).

Q: What fabric weights and widths are available for thobe fabric?

Our thobe fabric is available in weights from 100 GSM (grams per square meter) ultra-lightweight summer fabric to 220 GSM heavier-weight winter fabric. Our most popular weights for the Saudi market are 120–150 GSM for lightweight summer thobes and 170–200 GSM for winter and formal/dress thobes. Standard roll width is 150 cm (approximately 59 inches), which is the industry standard width for efficient thobe tailoring with minimal fabric waste. We can also supply 160 cm widths for specific cutting requirements or plus-size garment production. Each roll typically contains 50–100 meters of continuous fabric, wound on sturdy cardboard cores with protective outer wrapping to prevent soiling during transit.

Q: Do you offer wrinkle-resistant or easy-care thobe fabric finishes?

Yes, our polyester-cotton blend thobe fabrics feature inherent wrinkle-resistance properties from the synthetic fiber content. The 80/20 and 65/35 poly-cotton blends maintain a crisp white appearance throughout a full day of wear with minimal to no ironing required — simply hang after laundering. For enhanced performance, we can apply specialized finishing treatments during the textile manufacturing process: anti-static finish (reduces dust attraction and fabric cling in dry Saudi climates), moisture-wicking treatment (draws perspiration away from the skin for increased comfort in hot weather), and stain-release finish (makes oil and water-based stains easier to remove during laundering). All finishing chemicals meet OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certification for human ecological safety, ensuring the fabric is skin-safe for all-day wear.

Q: What happens if products arrive damaged or defective?

We stand behind our product quality and have a clear, fair claims resolution process. Step 1: Document any damage immediately upon container opening with clear photographs and video — this documentation is essential for both our investigation and any insurance claim. Step 2: Notify your account manager within 7 calendar days of delivery with detailed evidence of the issue. Step 3: For verified manufacturing defects, we offer replacement products shipped in your next order or a credit memo applied to your next purchase — whichever you prefer. Step 4: For shipping-related damage, we assist you with the marine cargo insurance claim process against the shipping line's liability. We strongly recommend that all sea freight shipments carry marine cargo insurance (costing approximately 0.3–0.5% of the cargo value) for full protection. Our overall defect rate across all product categories is consistently below 0.5%, a result of our rigorous multi-stage quality control process.
