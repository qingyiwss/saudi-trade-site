Professional Makeup Brushes

化妆刷 — Cosmetic Brush Sets

Hebei Province produces over 70% of the world's cosmetic brushes, with a mature supply chain spanning synthetic bristle production, ferrule manufacturing, handle crafting, and final assembly. Our OEM/ODM service allows Saudi beauty brands and distributors to create fully customized brush sets — from bristle density to handle design, packaging, and branding. All brushes use high-grade synthetic fibers that are cruelty-free, soft, and durable. Perfect for Saudi Arabia's growing beauty and personal care market.

Our factory is located in Qing County, Cangzhou, Hebei — the global capital of cosmetic brush manufacturing. This single county produces over 70% of the world's makeup brushes, backed by a 30-year supply chain ecosystem. From synthetic bristle extrusion to ferrule stamping, handle turning, and final assembly — every step happens within a 50km radius, ensuring consistent quality, fast turnaround, and competitive pricing that no other region can match.
